We will use nested loop to solve most of the pattern problems

Nested Loop:- Loop inside a loop
for() => outer loop
{
    for() => inner loop
}

eg. To Print below pattern, we will follow below steps
*
**
***
****

Steps:
1) Check No. of lines to be printed
=> here it is Four, so OUTER LOOP will run 4 times.

2) Check How many no. of times a character/symbol is printed
=> here it is printing the same no. of times the no. of line. i.e. Second line has 2 * printed

3) What we are printing
=> here we are printing *
